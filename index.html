/ 01
the show

We sit down with someone who's built an agent doing useful work in their business. We look at how they did it, then come back the following week to talk about what the rest of us could do with the same idea.

Neither of us is a trained developer, which is rather the point. We're interested in what people who can't really write code are still managing to build.

/ 02
the format

Each interview gets a follow-up the next week, plus a short write-up.

  • / 01
    the interview An interview with the person who built the agent. We get into how they made it, what's in their stack, what they tried that didn't work, what it actually does day to day, and what they'd do differently today.
  • / 02
    the follow-up A week later we come back to talk about what we'd take from it, and how we'd try to apply it ourselves.
  • / 03
    the write-up A short note on Substack pulling out the practical takeaways.
/ 03
who it's for

For leaders and operators putting AI to work in their organisations. People who are short on time, or hit a wall the moment things get super technical, and want more than another slick demo that falls apart on contact with real work.
